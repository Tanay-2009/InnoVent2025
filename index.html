Innovent

Connecting Biotech Startups with Investors

The Problem

Biotech startups struggle to find aligned investors who understand their science, and investors lack biotech-specific deal intelligence. This inefficiency delays innovation and limits funding opportunities for groundbreaking therapies.

Our Solution

Innovent is a biotech-focused investment marketplace that connects startups with investors using curated matchmaking and deep industry insights, including clinical trial progress, FDA approvals, and funding history.

Key Features

FDA Approval Tracking

Stay updated on regulatory milestones to assess biotech startup potential.

Funding History

Analyze past investment trends in the biotech space.

Mechanism of Action (MoA)

Understand the science behind biotech innovations for better decision-making.

Investor Matchmaking

Connect with investors aligned with your therapeutic focus and stage.

Clinical Trial Insights

Access real-time data on ongoing trials to evaluate biotech startups.
